Raspberry Smoothie

Description

Oh, raspberry smoothie, simplest of delights. This thing has always been there for me, bringing just the tiniest little hint of light to the darkest days.

A lot of smoothies are inedible to me because of their ingredients. I am strongly averse to bananas, and therefore, I basically cannot buy premade smoothies. My raspberry smoothie recipe is simple and elegant, using only four ingredients, and it's unbelievably refreshing. I love this thing, and I hope you will too!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen raspberries
  • 1 cup natural yoghurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Drizzle of honey

Method

  1. Put all the ingredients in the blender.
  2. Blend.
  3. Check that it has mixed completely and doesn't contain large lumps. If it does, go for another round of blending.
  4. Pour into a cup and enjoy!
